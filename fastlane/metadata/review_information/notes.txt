MornDash uses Apple's Family Controls / Screen Time APIs to block user-selected apps during a daily morning window until the user completes their task list.

How to test:
1. Complete onboarding and grant Screen Time permission when prompted.
2. Select 1–2 apps to block (e.g. Safari, Instagram) and set the start time to 1–2 minutes from now.
3. Add at least one simple task (e.g. "Stretch").
4. Wait for the start time — selected apps should show Apple's shield screen even if MornDash is closed.
5. Open MornDash, complete all tasks — the shield should lift and blocked apps become usable.
6. Alternative: tap "Give up" on the blocking screen and slide to confirm — shield lifts for the rest of the day.

Subscription (Pro):
- No demo account required. Use a Sandbox Apple ID to test in-app purchases.
- Free plan limits: 2 blocked apps, 3 tasks, 1 timer task. Pro removes these limits.

Camera (optional):
- Workout and Focus tasks use the front camera on-device only (ML pose/face detection). No video is recorded or uploaded.

No login is required. All data stays on device except anonymous analytics (Firebase) and purchase info (RevenueCat).
