MornDash makes your phone useless every morning until you actually finish your morning routine.

Set a wake-up time. From that moment, the distracting apps you choose are blocked. The block doesn't lift for an alarm, a snooze, or a burst of willpower — it lifts only when you check off every task on your morning list. No sound, no notifications, no shortcuts. Just you, your routine, and a phone that won't let you doomscroll in bed.

WHY MORNDASH
Most mornings die in the first ten minutes. You reach for your phone, open one app, and an hour is gone. MornDash removes the choice. Until your routine is done, Instagram, TikTok, X, YouTube and anything else you pick simply won't open.

HOW IT WORKS
- Pick the apps to block and a daily start time
- Every morning at that time, the block turns on automatically, even if the app is closed
- Complete your morning tasks (stretch, water, journal, whatever you set) to unlock
- Miss a day and your streak resets, so you stay honest

FEATURES
- App and category blocking powered by Screen Time
- Task-gated unlock: finish the list, get your phone back
- Daily streaks to build the habit
- Usage stats to see your progress
- 100% private: everything stays on your device
- No ads, no accounts, no alarms, no noise

WHO IT'S FOR
- People who doomscroll before getting out of bed
- Students and early risers building a morning routine
- Anyone fighting phone addiction or screen time overload
- Habit-builders who want real accountability, not another gentle reminder

Your morning sets the tone for your whole day. Make it count with MornDash.
